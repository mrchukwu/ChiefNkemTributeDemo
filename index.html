Biography

In Loving Memory

Brief history

25th Aug 1945 - 1st Jan 2022

Chief Goodhead Nkemakolam Nwachuku, the Ochije II of Egbema Clan, was born on the 25th of August 1945 to Eze J. N. A Nwachuku and Deaconess Pricillia Ejinwatuaka Nwachuku at Mgbede, Egbema, Rivers State. He started his early education at St. Paul's Primary School Ahoada and obtained his First School Leaving certificate. He then attended Okrika Grammar School ( OGS ) from 1959, where he was part of the school hockey team.
In 1966, he gained admission into the University of Nigeria, Nsukka and graduated with Second Class Upper Honours Degree in Accounting.

He started his career as Auditor II in the Rivers State Public Service where he worked until his successful retirement in 2005, attaining the position of the Auditor General of Rivers State. Being passionate about his service to his state, Chief Goodhead Nkem Nwachuku offered his early part of his retirement years as he accepted the appointment to serve as Commissioner in the Rivers State House of Assembly Service Commission.

Chief Goodhead Nwachukwu was happily married to his lovely wife Agatha ( fondly called Agat ). He was blessed with five children and nine grandchildren. Until his death, Chief was a loving husband, caring father, amazing brother, awesome grandpa, great uncle, wonderful in-law, friend and a jolly good fellow.

Survived by his beloved wife Mrs. Agatha Nwachuku

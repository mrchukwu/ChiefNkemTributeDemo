Chief Goodhead

Little history

25th Aug 1945 - 1st Jan 2022

Chief Goodhead Nkem Nwachuku, was born on the 25th of August 1945 to chief James and Mrs. Pricillia Nwachuku at Mgbede Egbema, Rivers State. He started his early education at St. Paul's Primary school ahoada and obtained his Standard Six ( First School Leaving certificate ). He then attended Okirika Grammar School ( OGS ), were he was part of the school criket team.
He gained admission into the university of Nigeria, Nsukka and graduated with a BSC in Accounting.
Chief Goodhead Nkem Nwachukwu started his career as a civil servant at the River State secretariate were he worked until his succeful retirement attaining the position of the Auditor General of Rivers State. Being passionate about his service ti his state, Chief Goodhead Nkem Nwachuku offered his early part of his retirement years as he accepted the appointment to serve as the Chairman of National Assembly Commission.
Chief Goohead was happily married to his lovely wife ( Agatha ) Agat as he would call her. He was blessed with five ( 5 ) children until his death, Chief was a loving husband, carring father, amazing brother, awesome grandpa,great uncle, wonderful in-law, friend and a Jolly good fellow.
